ESP32-C61-DevKitC-1
=====================

:link_to_translation:`zh_CN:[中文]`

ESP32-C61-DevKitC-1 is an entry-level development board based on ESP32-C61-WROOM-1, a general-purpose module featuring up to 8 MB SPI flash and 2 MB PSRAM. This board integrates complete Wi-Fi and Bluetooth® Low Energy functions.
